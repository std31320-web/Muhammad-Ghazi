# Global Superstore Performance Dashboard — Profitability Leak Analysis

## 🎯 The Problem
Global retail enterprises processing massive order volumes frequently suffer from "profitability leaks"—where high top-line revenues mask underlying inefficiencies. For Global Superstore, management lacked a centralized business intelligence interface to track how aggressive regional discounting structures and escalating shipping costs across diverse categories (Beauty, Tech, Fashion, Home) were directly eroding net margins.

## 🛠️ The Technical Action
* Engineered a multi-page **Power BI** executive dashboard connecting cross-border transactional data, regional logistics, and product category performance metrics.
* Developed a granular product diagnostics view leveraging card-KPI aggregates (`Profit per Unit`, `Average Discount Given`, `Shipping Cost per Unit`) to isolate underperforming products.
* Utilized complex **DAX measures** to calculate regional profit distributions, average freight margins, and order volume weights across 5 global macro-regions (APAC, Europe, LATAM, MEA, North America).
* Formatted the layout with high contrast, conditional design elements, and interactive category cross-filters to ensure immediate scannability of structural losses.

## 🚀 The Result
The dashboard successfully diagnosed critical margin killers: while the APAC region drove peak order volume, **aggressive wholesale discounting in APAC and disproportionately high shipping costs in MEA/LATAM were causing severe margin erosion, pushing 15% of total orders into near-zero or negative profitability.** Strategic actions implemented based on these insights include shipping strategy optimization in MEA, a complete restructuring of APAC wholesale discount caps, and a targeted review of the high-return Fashion category.
